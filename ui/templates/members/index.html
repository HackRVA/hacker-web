hack.rva has been in Richmond since 2009.

hack.rva is a Richmond-based hacker collective. We like to take things apart, put them back together, figure out how they work, and create new things.Public hack and project night every Thursday night at 7:30. Come see us!

Mission

To learn and build. We share tools and knowledge in technology, including Arduino, Makerbot, Linux, and the Open Source movement.

Description

We are located in the same building as The Dairy Bar, at Roseneath and Moore. Our door is facing the parking lot in the back of the building, off of Moore Street. You can park there too.
